  • Part I

    KISEIN is a premium bag brand to act properly. Under the slogan of OPEN TO ALL, KISEIN promises to fulfill the following four values ​​while delivering wonderful products.

    OPEN COST. We make a single product, open transparently the material cost, labor cost and operation cost that occurs to deliver the product to the customer.
    OPEN PRODUCT. Not only the visible material, but also the invisible material, using only the finest, all the origin of this material is open.
    OPEN FACTORY. Bag Making Experience We promise high quality products made by domestic artisans over 30 years and open the production process.
    OPEN HEART. To make the world a better place, we donate 5% of the proceeds from every product sold.

    KISEIN is a brand name based on English initials of family members of a brand founder.

    Jacklyn is the Founder, CEO and Creative Director of KISEIN. She conceptualized KISEIN with an aim to build a brand that, more than anything, is open and honest. She is responsible for the brand in every way – from design and manufacturing to marketing and sales.

    Jacklyn was an Architecture student and a designer at heart, who graduated with an Economics degree from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). She also received her Masters in Business Administration (MBA) from Columbia University. Jacklyn spent the first 18 years of her career as a business executive, working for major global companies, such as Motorola, Dell, Bain & Co., and Amorepacific. Her functional specialties include marketing, sales, and strategy, but it is her creative work in some major marketing campaigns, especially, that has won many accolades.

    When not designing or running KISEIN, Jacklyn can be found traveling or volunteering at her daughter’s school. She also serves as an Education Counselor for her alma mater, MIT, and is an active supporter of the Make A Wish foundation. An avid reader, her current topic of interest is Psychology. 

It is a brand that is created because it has a personal meaning, and as the name of the family is the brand, we promise to grow well in the future.
